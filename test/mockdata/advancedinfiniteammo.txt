                				<b><font size="4">Advanced Infinite Ammo</font></b><br />
 <br />
<b>Description: </b>Give Infinite Ammo/Clip/and more to everyone in game or on a specific target(s)<br />
 <br />
<b>List of features:</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Ammo/Clip</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Crit-a-cola/Bonk!</b> Note: Right-click(alt-fire) to manually remove bonk!<br />
<b>* Infinite Hype</b><br />
<b>* Infinite usage for Rage based weapons(Banners, Phlog, etc)</b><br />
<b>* Beggar's Bazooka - Hold right-click(alt-fire) to fire infinite rockets</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Manmelter/Frontier Justice/Diamondback Crits (Shoot once to activate)</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Shield Charge</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Ullapool Caber</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Metal/Sentrygun Ammo</b><br />
<b>* Infinite usage for Uber/Kritz/QuickFix</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Jarate/Sandman Balls/Sandviches/Madmilk/etc</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Cloak/Dead Ringer/Instant Recharge Spy-cicle</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Heads/Decapitations</b><br />
<b>* Infinite Spell Uses(Press reload to remove current spell)</b><br />
 <br />
 <br />
<b>CVARs(Default Values Shown: 1 = Enabled, 0 = Disabled):</b><br />
<b>Note: The plugin automatically creates AdvancedInfiniteAmmo.cfg at tf/cfg/sourcemod<br />
Change the default values there.</b><br />
sm_aia_version &quot;1.5.3&quot;<br />
<br />
sm_aia_all &quot;0&quot; - Advanced Infinite Ammo for everyone(and will automatically give it to anyone who connects)<br />
sm_aia_adminonly &quot;0&quot; - Advanced Infinite Ammo will work for admins only, 1 = Completely admin only, 2 = Admin only but the commands will work on non-admins<br />
     -Default &quot;b&quot; flag - Can be overridden with sm_aia_adminflag<br />
sm_aia_bots &quot;1&quot; - Advanced Infinite Ammo will work for bots<br />
sm_aia_roundwin &quot;1&quot; - Advanced Infinite Ammo for everyone on round win<br />
sm_aia_waitingforplayers &quot;1&quot; - Advanced Infinite Ammo for everyone during waiting for players phase<br />
sm_aia_chat &quot;1&quot; - Show Advanced Infinite Ammo changes in chat<br />
sm_aia_log &quot;1&quot; - Log Advanced Infinite Ammo commands<br />
sm_aia_disabledweapons &quot;&quot; - Weapons indexes to not give infinite ammo, separated by semicolons(<b>Do not add spaces</b>) --eg. sm_aia_disabledweapons &quot;44;58;222&quot; would disable sandman, jarate and mad milk<br />
<br />
sm_aia_clip &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Clip will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_ammo &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Ammo will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_extrastuff &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Extra Stuff will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_metal &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Metal will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_sentryammo &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Sentry Ammo will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_cloak &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Cloak will be globally disabled<br />
sm_aia_spells &quot;1&quot; - Infinite Spells will be globally disabled<br />
 <br />
<b>Commands:</b><br />
sm_aia &quot;target&quot; &quot;1/0&quot; - Give Advanced Infinite Ammo to the target(s) (Can auto-target self with just sm_fia)<br />
sm_aia2 &quot;target&quot; &quot;time(in seconds)&quot; - Give Advanced Infinite Ammo to the target(s) for a limited time<br />
sm_advanced_infinite_ammo &quot;target&quot; &quot;1/0&quot; - Same as sm_aia<br />
sm_advanced_infinite_ammo_timed &quot;target&quot; &quot;time(in seconds)&quot; - Same as sm_aia2<br />
sm_aiamenu - Open menu to choose target's AIA capabilities(flags globally disabled by the cvars cannot be changed)<br />
<b>Note</b>: Can override sm_aia_targetflag to control which admins can target others<br />
<br />
<b>Command Usage Examples:</b><br />
sm_aia              -Toggle AIA on yourself<br />
sm_aia Bob 1      -Enable AIA on Bob<br />
sm_aia @all 1     -Enable AIA on everyone<br />
sm_aia @red 1    -Enable AIA on red team<br />
sm_aia @!me 0   -Disable AIA for everyone except yourself<br />
(sm_advanced_infinite_ammo can be used in place of sm_aia)<br />
<br />
sm_aia2 @me 10 - Give yourself AIA for 10 seconds<br />
sm_aia2 Bob 30 - Give Bob AIA for 30 seconds<br />
sm_aia2 @all 300 - Give everyone AIA for 5 minutes<br />
(sm_advanced_infinite_ammo_timed can be used in place of sm_aia2)<br />
